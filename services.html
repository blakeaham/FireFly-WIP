hoop house design & build

If you are looking to expand your growing season, or get a jump on summer crops, season extension though high tunnels or hoop houses is a great place to start. We can design and source a structure to fit your space and needs. Let us help you find grant sources and navigate the grant application process. Our dedicated crew will build your tunnel on site and offer consultation for crop planning to make the most of your new extended season!
