Food is Social Justice.

Firefly Farm launched a commercial growing operation in 2015 with tomatoes and a few other summer crops. Selling to just three restaurant partners, the organically grown produce made fast friends in the local Wichita food community. In the intervening years, we have added almost 20 additional restaurants partners. While heirloom tomatoes are still at the heart of our business, we now grow a variety of crops in four seasons.
