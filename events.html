spring plant sale

With Spring comes growth, plant sales are fun and we are going to sell you plants! Grow fresh and local tomato plants, herbs, and flowers in your home gardens. These starters will make it easy for you to host your own garden-to-table dinners in no time. Come by and talk to other home gardeners like yourself and bring home some new plants and ideas.

summer farm-to-table dinners

Farm-to-table dinners have been held here in our barns since 2016 to rave reviews. We source fruits and vegetables from our own farm and supplement with ingredients from other local farms and food makers. Then we invite local guest chefs to turn these farm-fresh, locally-sourced ingredients into one-of-a-kind, five-course meals. We pair these courses with the best of locally-made beer, wines, and spirits to round out a truly unique farm-to-fork experience. Watch our Facebook page to learn about upcoming events.

fall leaf festival

Did you know that yard waste, like leaves and trimmings, makes up almost 13% of landfill waste? During our last Fall leaf drive we put the call out to all our friends and neighbors to help divert some of that organic waste, and help build our soil fertility through our compost program. We were thrilled by the popularity of this idea and were quickly overwhelmed with donations!
- thank you, Wichita!

We gathered leaves from many neighborhoods, including: Overbrook, Crestview, Hawthorne, Lakepoint, Vickridge, Eastborough, Woodlawn Village, and Riverside. We had one kind friend in College Hill organize her whole block! We especially want to thank our friends at Nudge Compost for putting the word out to their customers. Another thank you goes out to Tree Top Nursery as they dumped their leaves here for a couple of weeks, too.
